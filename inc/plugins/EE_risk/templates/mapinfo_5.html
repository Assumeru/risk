Benelux + Germany

This map has a total of 40 territories divided over 4 players.

Victory Conditions

There are three different missions on this map.

  • Benelux

    To win you will have to conquer the Kingdom of Belgium, the Netherlands, and the Grand Duchy of Luxembourg.

  • Greater Germany

    To win you will have to conquer the Federal Republic of Germany and one additional region.

  • Rivalry

    To win you will have to conquer the last of another player's territories. Should you fail, you will have to conquer 24 territories instead.
