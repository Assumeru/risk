With AutoRoll enabled the system will decide the number of dice used in defense. If the attacker only attacks with one die, you will defend with two. If the average of the highest two attacking dice rolls is equal to or less than 3.5, you will defend with two dice. If the second highest attacking die is less than 4, you will defend with two dice. Note, if you can only defend with one die, or if the attacker rolls only one die and it's 1, the system will roll for you.
