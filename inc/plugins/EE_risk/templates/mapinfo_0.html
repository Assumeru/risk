Benelux

The Benelux is a union between the European countries of Belgium, the Netherlands, and Luxembourg. Founded in the 1940s, the Benelux predates the European Union.

The in-game map is composed of the provinces of Belgium, the Brussels-Capital Region, the provinces of the Netherlands, and Luxembourg. These 24 territories are divided between the two players, the game is won when only one player is left.
