North America

The real name of this map is the Members of the North American Free Trade Agreement & Cuba. NAFTA is a trade agreement between Canada, Mexico and the United States of America established in 1994. Along with Cuba these three countries give this map a total of 96 territories divided between six players.

Victory Conditions

There are five different missions on this map.

  • Canadian Expansion

    To win you will have to conquer Canada and 35 other territories. (48 territories in total.)

  • Native Power

    To win you will have to conquer the United Mexican States and the Iroquois League. (40 territories in total.)

  • Old Times

    To win you will have to conquer the Viceroyalty of New Spain and the Confederated States of America. (50 territories in total.)

  • Rivalry

    To win you will have to conquer the last of another player's territories. Should you fail, you will have to conquer 50 territories instead.

  • Uncle Sam

    To win you will have to conquer all 50 states of the United States of America.
